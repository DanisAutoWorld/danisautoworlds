How To Choose the Right Brake Pads for Your Vehicle

Kunle A.

Technical Lead
Jun 1, 2025 • 8 Comments

Brake pads are vital for your car’s safety. But with so many options available, how do you pick the right one for your model and driving style?

When it comes to replacing your brake pads, quality and compatibility matter most. Understanding the different types—ceramic, semi-metallic, organic—and their benefits will help you make an informed choice.

Types of Brake Pads

  • Ceramic: Quiet, long-lasting, less dust, best for city drivers.
  • Semi-Metallic: Great for performance, conducts heat well, more durable for heavy-duty use.
  • Organic: Affordable and gentle on rotors, but wears quicker.

Expert Tips

  • Always check your car’s manual for compatibility
  • Ask about warranty and OE standards
  • Install with a trusted technician for best result

When to Replace Brake Pads

Squealing sounds, reduced braking power, vibration, or a warning light are all signs it’s time for new pads.
